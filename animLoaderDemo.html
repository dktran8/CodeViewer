Fan Control

When the unit is enabled to run, the fan will be commanded to start, running continuously, modulating its speed to maintain the duct static pressure setpoint 1.00 WC.

The unit will monitor the associated terminal unit damper positions and look at the damper in position 1 when sorted from most to least open. Current Reading: 0.0 %

The static pressure will be reset in increments of 0.20 WC every 00:04:00 to achieve a damper position of 90.0 % with a deadband of 5.0 %.

The static pressure minimum will be set at 0.50 WC, the maximum at 1.95 WC and initial at 1.00 WC. Current Reset Status: OptimumSP
